Association

  • Association

    Several student centered activities were organized by the Architecture Association. Photography club, Documentation cell, Cultural and Creative club are functioning effectively in the Association to make every event successful. As a part of the association, various fests, workshops and seminars have been conducted for the students. Every year a national level symposium "ARK 2K" is organized in the in which students from other Institutions are invited to participate in various technical and non-technical events.
