Student Works

  • As a part of B.Arch curriculum case study visits and trips have been organized for the benefit of the students. South India and North India Tours are arranged every year. Case study visits are arranged to Bengaluru, Hyderabad ,Chennai, Pondicherry, Karaikudi and other places of cultural and historic importance places. Also study trips are organized to places in and around Erode District and Coimbatore as a part of their learning enhancement.
