ELIGIBILITY

  • Eligibility

    Pass in 10+ 2 Examination with 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics and also at least 50% marks in aggregate of the 10 + 2 Examination or passed 10+3 Diploma Examination with Mathematics as compulsory subject with at least 50% marks in aggregate.

    Qualified in NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture)
